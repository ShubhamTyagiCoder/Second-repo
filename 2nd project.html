ChartViewer is a social media network[1], analysis platform[2][3] and mobile app[4] for traders and investors[5]. The company was founded in 2011[6] and today has offices in New York, London[7] and Russia. In 2018, ChartViewer completed a $37 million Series B[8] round led by Insight Partners, and in 2019 they acquired TradeIT[9]. To date, the company ranks in the top 130 websites globally according to Alexa[10], and has over 10 million active monthly users[11].

-Stock, Futures and Foreign exchange forex charting and trading.
